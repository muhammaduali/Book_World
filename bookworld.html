Pride and Prejudice

Jane Austen

First published in 1813, this romantic novel of manners is one of the most beloved books in English literature. It follows Elizabeth Bennet, one of five sisters, as she navigates issues of marriage, morality, and misunderstandings in British landed gentry society. The book is known for its witty commentary and complex characters, particularly Elizabeth and Mr. Darcy.
